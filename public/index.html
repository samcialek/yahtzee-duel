Variant · how you share the dice

How much of the win each variant hands to luck rather than skill — how often perfect play beats near-perfect play across thousands of simulated games. 100% skill means the better play always wins; 0% means a coin flip. The figure on the right of each card is how many games it takes before perfect play is 95% likely to have the higher combined score — the fewer, the more skill decides it.
